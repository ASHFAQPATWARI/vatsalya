FLAVORS TO SUIT YOUR TASTES

Global Cuisine

From the Rajasthani Thali to Italian Pizza

Relish our Chef’s signature dishes

Sit back and let our chefs find flavors that please your palate and bring you delectable food experiences. Crafted with care and handled with utmost hygiene, we are sure you will enjoy our mouthwatering dishes that include Indian, Continental, Asian, European and fusion recipes. Enjoy the harmony of fresh, seasonal produce and sustainable food selections. You can choose to be served in the dining area, pool side, lounge, gardens or under the mango tree. Prepare to be pampered like never before.

If cooking relaxes you, feel free to walk into our kitchen to whip up your favorite dishes. Our team will stand by with assistance and a smile. We also host cooking demonstrations and workshops to learn Indian cooking.

  • Hygienically prepared
  • Professionally trained kitchen team
  • Made with fresh locally sourced produce
  • Meals made to your order
  • Barbeque and Bonfire
  • Cook in our walk-in kitchen
  • On-demand cooking workshops with lunches
