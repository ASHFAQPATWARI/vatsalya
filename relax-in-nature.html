BREATHE IN THE PEACE

Unwind in Nature

Inhale peace, exhale happiness

Feel the Vatsalya Vihar vibe

As you walk into the gates, you will find yourself embraced by a unique sense of peace and tranquillity. Our thousand plus trees create an ecosystem of freshness in which a variety of birds, small animals, plants and flowers thrive. You will find a marked difference in the temperature and quality of air that you breathe here. Take long walks, naps in the hammocks, hear the calling of birds, listen to the breeze sweeping across leaves, sip on fresh drinks and relax into a never before feeling of calm.

We are blessed to have flowers blooming almost all year round. You will enjoy these bounties of nature as well as the earthy touches like our mud and thatch gazebo. If workation is your idea of a holiday, you will find the peace you need to accelerate your productivity and creativity.

  • 1000 + trees
  • Fruit orchards
  • Home to migratory birds
  • Fresh clean air
  • Organic vegetable patch
  • Sit Outs, Hammocks and Gazebos
  • Peaceful and Quiet for a Workation
