HAVE AN ADVENTURE IN NATURE

Outdoor Games & Activities

There is never a dull moment at Vatsalya Vihar. We offer a range of outdoor activities to make your holiday as action-packed as you desire. A walking track wraps around the property and we provide cycles in all sizes for you to ride around the 10 acres of greenery. The private flood-lit cricket pitch is a rare delight for our guests. The little ones also enjoy playing badminton, running around in the gardens and a game of football. Come together over a game of mini-golf or croquet or go for a picnic to the nearby Badi lake in our vintage WW-II jeep. Discover your personal paradise at Vatsalya Vihar.

  • Walking, Running, Jogging Track
  • Flood-lit Cricket Pitch
  • Gardens for badminton, football
  • Mini Golf, Croquet, Cycling
  • Badi lake picnic, village walk, Vintage jeep ride
  • Golf Cart Access Throughout the Property
  • Visit and Volunteer at Animal Aid (1 km away)
