ENJOY THE RARE LUXURY OF

Glorious Nature, City Adventure And the freedom to be you!

Aravali sunrises, breakfast under trees, a trip to the city palace and boat ride, back to a relaxing swim, evening massage and bonfire dinner spiced with farm-fresh goodness - now that’s a dream holiday.


Live it at Vatsalya Vihar luxury retreat in Udaipur, where you are close (just 2o minutes) to the city’s centre, yet far enough from its tourist bustle and noise. Unlike multi-room properties or confined homestays, we are a boutique retreat of 7 full-service villas in 10 lush acres. In the lap of 1,000 trees, overlooking hills, our attentive hand-picked team ensures you never feel one-in-a- crowd or cut off from care.

Oh, and we love it when you bring your pets along!

Our 24/7 services with no time-bound rules or closing hours let you (for once) experience a pampered vacation at your pace, in your time, to your taste.

INDULGE IN THE EXTRAORDINARY

Udaipur’s only Private Heated Pool Villas

Dive into the personalized luxurious warmth of your private heated pool attached to your elegant villa. You can soak and sit or take laps and swim. Choose to make it unforgettable. After all, we are much more than a plunge pool room, we are a pool villa, with a 1000 sq feet deck, lounge area, patio- style seating and a pool that’s big enough for a party!

24/7 indulgence that’s all for you and your special ones.

Discover a new standard of luxury at Vatsalya Vihar Boutique Retreat Udaipur – where every detail is designed to exceed your desires.

VATSALYA VIHAR BOUTIQUE RETREAT UDAIPUR

Where Serenity Meets Sophistication

Vatsalya Vihar is a unique passion project – a luxury retreat in Udaipur where you’ll experience the lavishness of top-notch hotels minus their fixed rules and formalities.
