Swimming Pool

Nothing brings people together like pool time

Make a splash on your next vacation

Our pool remains open 24/7 so you can make the most of your time here. The expansive pool sits at the heart of Vatsalya Vihar. Flanked by the 70 year old mango tree on one side and the open gardens on the other, the poolside is a perfect place to sunbathe, dine, lounge and watch the world go by. The 55 feet pool is large enough for laps and pool games while being safe to swim at leisure. Our young guests especially enjoy the splash pool. We maintain the highest level of hygiene and cleanliness so you can enjoy your time here with the comfort and care that you deserve.

  • 24/7 open pool
  • 55 ft lap pool
  • Kids splash pool
  • Pool games
  • Pool side dining
