THE ENCHANTING

Pomello Villa

Dressed in charcoal grey, earthy brown and complementary cream, the glorious Pomello Villa brings together enchanting hues. Named after the Pomello tree with its citrusy flavor and juicy goodness.

The Villa boasts a master bedroom with an attached living room that offers extra space and privacy to guests. The lavish balcony overlooks the landscaped gardens, tree lined pathway and the swimming pool. Here, you can relax and just watch the world go by, see nature’s magic unfold and indulge in the peace and privacy you deserve.

The stone finish bathroom with a rain shower, choicest of toiletries and soft towels will pamper you.

Step in for comfort and privacy like you have never experienced before.
