Vatsalya Vihar - A Luxury Pool Villas Resort

Luxury Retreat In Udaipur

Vatsalya Vihar is a luxury retreat located in Udaipur, India. It is a premium resort that offers a serene and tranquil environment for guests to relax and unwind. The resort features a range of luxurious and spacious rooms and suites, each one designed with traditional Indian decor and modern amenities.

The luxury resort features a large outdoor pool, a well-stocked gym, a spa, and many dining options both Indian as well as other cuisines. Additionally, guests may take advantage of a variety of activities and adventures, such as yoga and meditation sessions, guided tours of local attractions, and relaxing boat rides on Lake Pichola.

This luxury suites Located just a few kilometers from the city center. Vatsalya Vihar provides easy access to Udaipur's various attractions, including as its beautiful palaces, temples, and museums. Vatsalya Vihar is a popular choice for travelers to Udaipur because to its combination of elegance, comfort, and traditional Indian charm.

LABOUR OF LOVE

Vatsalya Vihar is the brainchild of hotelier couple Vandana and Vagmi

The retreat, which was a weekend home of the couple, has been thoughtfully expanded into a one-of-its-kind holiday destination for nature lovers, luxury travelers and experience seekers.

Professional Team

We are more than a team, we are family

Our team goes the extra mile to make your stay as comfortable as possible. Led by our talented manager Ashok Akhawat, our team of chefs, hospitality staff, servers and gardeners will make your holiday here most memorable and enjoyable.

PET-FRIENDLY

We are animal lovers and happily welcome pets

Trained pets are welcome at Vatsalya Vihar. Our furry friends will enjoy the open spaces and fresh air here as they frolick and have pawsome fun.

CONSERVATION

We are passionate about making nature-friendly choices

Vatsalya Vihar is entirely powered by solar and we use energy-efficient light fixtures. Composting is very important to us to create farm fresh manure for the ample vegetation we proudly nurture here.

LOCAL ART & SKILLS

Supporting the indigenous community is a priority for us

We strive to preserve local skills and art. From the local cuisine, blue pottery china to the handcrafted tiles in our pool and the stone used in our wall facades, we actively use local skills and raw materials to support the regional community.

SUSTAINABILITY

Upcycled pallet wood furniture is our specialty

We have handcrafted many of our furniture pieces from recycled wood. You will see it in our patio pieces, pool deck chairs, planters and picket fences. Feel free to ask us if you wish to learn more about this recycling technique.
